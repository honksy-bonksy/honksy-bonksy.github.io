Blog Entry 002: The Alternative

(...get it?)


Hello again! It is I, the great Honketh Bonketh. Today I will be talking about a very helpful tool/website: AlternativeTo.net.
AlternativeTo is a neat ol’ site that gathers info on apps of all platforms (web, Windows, Mac & Linux) and licenses (Commercial, Freemium & Open Source) and proceeds to crowd-source information about alternatives to these pieces of software.
It’s very useful for finding things that are open-source, but do the same thing as X-Corp’s $100,000 pay-to-use monthly-subscription bleed-your-wallet-dry software.
You’ll not always end up with what you need, but it’s always worth a shot. It’s how I landed on a lot of different software that I now use regularly. That site in full is: https://www.AlternativeTo.net
